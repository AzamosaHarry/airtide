ABOUT AIRTIDE LOGISTICS

Airtide Logistics was established to provide professional logistics services to both local and international clients Our departments, professional staff, industry best practices and available tools cover customs clearance, overland transport as well as Sea and Air Freight services are the key to our success. We have the advantage of providing all services under one roof in addition to being members with International networks providing worldwide coverage & reliable agents & partners globally.
Our Vision is to provide long-term value to our clients and agents by consistently and openly delivering the client's products safely and on schedule, all while maintaining a culture of integrity and honesty that allows our firm to thrive. As an extension of your company, our goal is to exceed your expectations. We relish the challenge and look forward to assisting your company in its expansion.

We don't only handle your logistics; we also provide value to the rest of your supply chain. To move forward logically, we invest in knowledge, people, and the creation of an effective network, all while utilizing cutting-edge technology advancements.
